Privacy Policy for WonderTogether

Last Updated: December 23, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to WonderTogether ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Profile photos, group names, and relationship details you choose to share
  • Content: Photos, trip details, notes, and other content you create within the app
  • Group Information: Information about groups you create or join

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: When you grant permission, we collect location information to tag photos and display travels on the map
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the app to improve our services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the WonderTogether service
  • Enable you to share memories and plan trips with your groups
  • Display your travels on maps using location data
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Communicate with you about updates or issues
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Within Groups

When you join a group, your profile information, photos, trips, and other content you create are shared with all members of that group. This is the primary function of the app.

4.2 Service Providers

We use Supabase (supabase.com) as our backend service provider for:

  • User authentication
  • Database storage
  • File and photo storage

Supabase has its own privacy policy and maintains high security standards. Your data is stored securely and encrypted.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or government request, or to protect our rights and safety.

4.4 We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your photos for marketing without explicit permission
  • Track you across other apps or websites

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase's cloud infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted password storage
  • Row-level security on database
  • Secure authentication tokens

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Leave groups at any time

6.2 Permissions

You can control app permissions through your device settings:

  • Camera: Required to capture photos. Can be revoked anytime.
  • Photo Library: Required to upload and save photos. Can be revoked anytime.
  • Location: Optional. Used to tag photos and show travels on map. Can be revoked anytime.

6.3 Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. We will process your request within 30 days. Note that data you've shared in groups may remain visible to other group members until they delete it.

7. Children's Privacy

WonderTogether is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed in any country where Supabase operates. By using WonderTogether, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside your country of residence.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your information within 30 days, except where we must retain information for legal obligations.
