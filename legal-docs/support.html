Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?

After signing up, tap the "Create Group" button on the home screen. Give your group a name, and you'll receive a unique invite code to share with others.

How do I join an existing group?

Ask a group member for the invite code, then tap "Join Group" and enter the code. You'll instantly have access to all shared trips and memories.

Can I be part of multiple groups?

Yes! You can create and join multiple groups. Switch between them using the group selector in settings.

How do I add photos to a trip?

Open any trip, tap the "+" button, and select photos from your library or take new ones with your camera.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely! Only members of your group can see your trips and photos. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your data with third parties.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email us at luyasuseb@gmail.com with details. We love hearing from our users!
